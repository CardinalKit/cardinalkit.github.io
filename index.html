Why CardinalKit ?

Extensible

CardinalKit gives your project access to several recognized medical surveys, Bluetooth-wearable integration, and limitless data collection.

Efficient

Rather than building an entire team dedicated to building a research application, save money and time by using CardinalKit .

Open Source

Our entire project is available on Github and is maintained by a diverse community of researchers and developers.

Scalable

Our cloud implementation within CardinalKit leverages Google Cloud so that you can focus on your apps and participants rather than messy online infrastructure.

Modern

Our software is built using the most up-to-date packages, methodology, and technology. Your research apps will make you, and your participants, happy.

Community Driven

We want to build components that you actually want to use. Reach out to us if you have any features you would like to see.
